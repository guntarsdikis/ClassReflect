ClassReflect Feature Overview (For Management)
Last Updated: 2025-09-25

What ClassReflect Does
- Captures classroom audio (upload a file or record in the browser)
- Transcribes the lesson into readable text
- Applies an evaluation template to generate strengths, improvements, and an overall score
- Helps leaders manage schools, users, subjects, templates, and analyses

Who Uses ClassReflect
- Teacher: Uploads/records lessons and reviews own feedback and transcripts
- School Manager: Oversees teachers, templates, and school recordings and insights
- Super Admin: Manages multiple schools and users across the platform

Core Capabilities
- Upload & Record
  - One-page upload wizard with class details (subject, grade, duration, notes)
  - Built-in browser recorder with level meter and simple guidance
  - Large files supported via a reliable upload flow
- Transcription
  - High-quality automatic transcription of lesson audio
  - Transcript available for quick reading and review
- Teaching Analysis
  - Apply a template to a transcript to produce: overall score, key strengths, targeted improvements
- Templates & Frameworks
  - Create custom evaluation templates with weighted criteria (must total 100%)
  - Filter by category, subject, and grade
  - One-click import of “Teach Like a Champion” starter templates
- Dashboards & Reporting
  - Teacher Reports: simple personal view of recordings and results
  - School Manager overview: recent uploads, completion and scores at a glance
  - Export analysis reports to PDF for sharing
- Recordings & Playback
  - Searchable list with status, teacher, class, and date
  - Short-lived playback links for secure listening
  - View transcripts in a clean modal
- People & School Management
  - Add and manage teachers and managers
  - Reset passwords, activate or deactivate accounts
  - Manage school subjects and template categories

Typical Workflows
- Teacher: Record or upload → monitor status → review transcript → view feedback after analysis
- School Manager: Add teachers → import/create templates → upload or approve uploads → apply templates → review results and export PDFs
- Super Admin: Create schools → assign school managers → monitor platform activity and usage

Options & Customization
- Templates: Build multi-criterion rubrics with weights, organize by category/subject/grade, import TLC templates
- Subjects & Grades: Maintain a school-specific subjects list and consistent grade tagging

Security & Privacy (Plain English)
- Role-based access: teachers see their own items; managers see their school; super admins oversee the platform
- Short-lived links for audio playback; transcripts and analyses stored securely
- Password reset and invitation emails supported

What’s Available Today
- Ready now: uploads and recording, transcription, template-based analysis, role-based flows, PDF export, user/school/template management
- In progress / next: deeper school analytics and trends; more ready-made templates

Getting Started
- Sign in with your role (teacher, manager, super admin)
- Managers: add teachers and import a template
- Upload a recording (or record directly), then apply a template
- Review the feedback and export the report to PDF if needed
